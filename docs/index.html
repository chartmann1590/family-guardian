location_on

Live GPS Tracking

Background location reporting from the Android app with real-time marker updates on the web dashboard via WebSocket.

sensors

Geofence Alerts

Define safety places per circle. Get notified instantly when family members arrive or leave designated areas.

emergency

SOS Panic Button

One-tap emergency activation from the Android app. Pulsing red alert on the dashboard with last-known location fallback.

group

Family Circles

Organize members into circles with invite codes. The first user bootstraps as admin and manages membership.

notifications_active

Push Notifications

Android event stream with exponential-backoff reconnection. High-priority heads-up for SOS, standard for geofence events.

devices

Three Clients, Full Parity

Native Android (Kotlin + Compose), native iOS (Expo / React Native, sideloaded), and a mobile PWA at /app for any phone with a browser.

pause_circle

Pause Sharing

One-tap freeze of your last-known location for 15 min / 1 hr / 4 hr / "until 8 PM". The circle sees a ⏸ badge until you resume — never your live position.

visibility

Audit Log

See exactly who in your circle has viewed your history, visits, or trips — and when. You can only see views of you, never others.

file_download

Export & Delete

Download a JSON of everything the server stores about you, or delete your account with password re-confirm. Sole-admin deletes require promoting a successor first.

verified_user Privacy by Architecture

Your data never
leaves your server

Family Guardian is designed around a simple principle: location data is sensitive, and you should be the only one who holds it. There is no cloud backend, no analytics, no telemetry, and no third-party services involved in the core platform.

  • check_circle
    SQLite on your disk
    All data stored in a single file inside your Docker volume. You own the backup.
  • check_circle
    Argon2id password hashing
    Industry-standard password security. Session tokens are opaque and server-managed.
  • check_circle
    No external dependencies
    The entire stack runs in one container. No external databases, CDNs, or analytics.
  • check_circle
    Open source
    Full source code available for audit. Trust through transparency.
